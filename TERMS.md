## About Norah Speech

Norah Speech is a free, open-source Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) web application designed for pre-literate children. It is provided at no cost and with no ads.

## Acceptance of Terms

By using Norah Speech, you agree to these terms. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

## Use of the App

Norah Speech is intended to support communication for children with speech and language needs. You may use it freely for personal, educational, and therapeutic purposes.

**Norah Speech is not a medical device.** It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. It is a communication aid. Always consult qualified speech-language pathologists, therapists, or medical professionals for clinical guidance.

## Privacy & Data

**Norah Speech does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** All user data — including custom boards, photos, and settings — is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. No data ever leaves your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking.

- No server-side data storage
- No user accounts or authentication
- No analytics or usage tracking
- No cookies
- No advertising
